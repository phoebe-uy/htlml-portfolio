The Best TV Series of 2023 According to Phoebe

My Top 3 Picks

Tiny Beautiful Things

A TV mini series about a middle-aged woman who reluctantly ends up dishing life advice for an online column despite having trouble herself with adulting and keeping her life and family together.

Shrinking

A TV mini series about a recently-widowed therapist who starts to rebuild his life after a wayward stretch of debauchery. Frustrated with his patients' stagnation and lack of progress, he starts employing unconventional methods that start to incite changes in his patients' lives.

Pivoting

A TV series (now cancelled) about three friends in their forties who lose their best friend to cancer. The loss of their friend shakes their foundation, where the three of them are inspired to start seizing the day and make pivotal (sometimes whimsical) changes in their lives.
